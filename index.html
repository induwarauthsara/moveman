Move To Earn

MoveMan is an application developed, based on Move To Earn concept. It pushes the users to commit on physical activities such as running, walking, jogging etc. The motive would be the rewards the users could claim by engaging on those activities. Hence, it positively impacts the healthiness of the general public.

WHAT IS MOVEMAN?

MoveMan blend well with the Move-To-Earn concept and the platform which it is built upon matches well with the task as well. To win rewards, users should involve in outdoor activities such as walking, running, jogging etc.


Accordingly, the users can earn rewards at no expense. To clarify, MoveMan is designed in a way that the rewards are granted when they walk, run, jog, exercise etc. Users commit in these such activities anyway in day-to-day life, hence this a useful app for them to earn rewards without any extra effort. In other words, this app gives a reward value to daily activities that any user commits.

NFT Rent
